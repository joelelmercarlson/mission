# Mission Statement

The Academy of Technology (AoT) is an international group of dedicated
computing professionals who have a shared purpose and belief of making
computing better for everyone.

## Values

1. The promotion and advancement of Computing engineering and architecture.
1. The promotion and advancement of Computing solutions at scale.
1. The promotion and advancement of Computing as an international shared-responsibility.
1. Computing is a universal and basic human right. 

## Association

1. The members are world-class architects, engineers and developers which focus on really difficult problems.
1. The members architect, engineer and develop solutions in following domains:

<ul>
<li>disaster recovery
<li>business continuity
<li>data-governance
<li>artifical-intelligence
<li>cloud migration &amp; hosing
</ul>

1. Members and affiliates provide client services utilizing our AoT core capabilities.

## Who do we serve

1. Enterprise
1. Government
1. Non-Profit &amp; social causes
1. Education, students, users &amp individual computing enthusiasts.
